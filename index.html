About Us

Pink Pistols Denver is a Colorado-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to inclusive public safety education, community outreach, and violence prevention. Pink Pistols Denver is a local chapter of Pink Pistols, a national organization focused on education, community, and responsible firearm ownership.

Mission Statement

Pink Pistols Denver provides educational programming and community outreach to LGBTQ individuals, including public safety education, responsible firearm ownership training, and violence prevention resources to promote personal safety and reduce harm.

Programs & Resources

Firearm Safety Education

Educational programs focused on safe handling, secure storage, responsible ownership, and range safety fundamentals.

Violence Prevention

Community-centered approaches to harm reduction, emergency preparedness, and personal safety education.

LGBTQ Outreach

Inclusive educational resources and supportive community engagement for LGBTQ individuals and allies.
